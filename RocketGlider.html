Rocket Glider

Organization: Columbia University
Role: Student
Date: Dec 2020

As a student in Columbia University's Art of Engineering class (taught in part by Professor Mike Massimino), I collaborated with three other students to design a rocket glider. We did rapid research and modeling in Solidworks as we were only given 24 hours to make our design. Our glider involves flexible wings that can be stored during the rocket phase and deployed for the glide phase. Due to Covid-19 we were unable to test our design.
